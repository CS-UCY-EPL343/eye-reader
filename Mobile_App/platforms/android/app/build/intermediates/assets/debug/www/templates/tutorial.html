Edit Profile

1. Click "View your profile" in the side menu.

2. Click "Edit Profile" to edit your personal information.

3. Edit your information and save the changes.

Edit Profile

4. By checking the "Are you a journalist" you have the extra perk of adding personal notes, that only you can read and edit, to the articles.

Add Sources


1. Click "Add Sources" in the side menu.

2. Select sources to read articles from.

Save Article

1. Slide the small arrow '>' to the left.

2. Select the to save the article.

3. The article will be saved in the "Saved" in the side menu.

Report Article

1. Slide the small arrow '>' to the left.

2. Select the to report the article.

3. Check the reason you want to report the article.

View Statistics

1. Click "Statistics" in the side menu.

2. By fetching statistics it present the statistics of all the sources or the one you select.

3. Different type of statistics can be selected only for all sources.

View Statistics

4."View Statistics" (Report counter, Click counter and Setiment Analysis).

Settings

1. Click "Settings" in the side menu.

2. Enable night mode, change the app's font size and enable article caching for offline reading.

Settings

3. Enable "Markup Toxic" or "Hide Toxic" to highlight or hide toxic content.

4. Set your tolerance level against toxic content (the higher the value, the more tolerant you are).
