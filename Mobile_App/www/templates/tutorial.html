Edit Profile

1. Click your username in the side menu.

2. Click "Edit Profile" to edit your personal information.

2. Save all the changes..

Add Sources


1. Click "Add Sources" in the side menu.

2. Select sources to read articles.

Save Article

1. Slide the small arrow '>' next to the article.

2. Select the to save the article.

3. The article will be saved in the "Saved" in the side menu.

Report Article

1. Slide the small arrow '>' next to the article.

2. Select the to report the article.

3. Check the reason you want to report this article.

View Statistics

1. Click "Statistics" in the side menu.

3. To see the statistics of a source use the drop down "Select Sources".

View Statistics

4. To chance the statistic's type use the drop down "View Statistics".

Report: The percentage of reported articles of a source.

Hate speech: The percentage of hate speech in articles or in the comments of a source.

Sentiment Analysis: The percenage of negative, positive and neutral response.
