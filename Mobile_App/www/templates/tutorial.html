Add Sources


1. To add sources click on "Add Sources" in the side menu.

2. Select or deselct the sources that you want to read articles from.

Save Article

1. Slide the article from the small arrow '>' next to the article.

2. Select the in the options to save the article.

3. The article will be saved in the "Saved" in the side menu.

Report Article

1. Slide the article from the small arrow '>' next to the article.

2. Select the in the options to report the article.

3. Check in the pop-up the reason you want to report this article.
