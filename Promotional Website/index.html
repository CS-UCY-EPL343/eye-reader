We filter your news

A personal news reader tailored to your reading taste.

It delicately collects all the articles of the topics you most care about, considering your tolerance.

News on demand

  • It presents articles based on the user's preference of sources.
  • When online, it keeps the feed up to date in real time.
  • There is an option of cashe news for offline reading.

Hate and toxic free

  • It filters the articles' content based on user's tolerance.
  • It hides or marks any toxic or hate content in the articles.

Statistics

  • Initially it presents the top 5 most reported sources.
  • It presents the statistics of each source (Report percentage, Hate speech percentage and Sentiment Analysis).
